Where it all began

Missing the Taste of Home?

Udipti Mishthan was born from a simple feeling that many people in Mumbai share — the longing for authentic, home-cooked sweets that taste exactly like what your grandmother used to make.

We are a home-based mithai shop in Chembur, Mumbai, passionately crafting traditional Maharashtrian and Indian sweets with pure homemade ghee, natural ingredients, and most importantly — love.

Every ladoo we roll, every modak we shape, every peda we press — all done by hand, for you. We believe a sweet isn't just a sweet. It's a memory, a celebration, a hug on a plate.

No preservatives. No artificial flavours. Just pure, homemade goodness.

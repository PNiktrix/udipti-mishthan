🍬 Who We Are

The Udipti Mishthan Story

Udipti Mishthan was born from a simple belief — pure ingredients make the purest sweets. Founded with a passion for traditional Indian mithai, we have been crafting handmade sweets in the heart of Mumbai for over two decades.

Every sweet at Udipti Mishthan is made fresh each morning using time-honoured recipes passed down through generations. We never compromise on quality — no artificial colours, no preservatives, just natural goodness in every bite.

From classic Ladoo and Barfi to modern sugar-free & jaggery sweets, we cater to every palate — including health-conscious customers, diabetics, and those seeking vegan options.
